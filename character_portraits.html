Sometime in early 2020 I started taking requests online for portraits of fictional characters. This started as part of a popular challenge at the time called #sixfanarts, but quickly grew into its own thing. Here are the ones I've done so far. All are pen and ink linework with digital color.

I'm still taking requests but it's probably for the best that you send them via Instagram, and don't expect a quick turnaround.
