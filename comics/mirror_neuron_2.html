Mirror Neuron No. 2 is the second issue of my solo comics anthology magazine (previously published as Sanity Check). It contains two original short stories and a variety of bonus features, a total of 44 pages of content. Print copies have been produced in a numbered edition of 100.

Perhaps best of all, until 30 April 2022, Mirror Neuron will be distributed on a not-for-profit model. If you can't afford to pay, you can still get a copy--just checkout below for $0. You will still receive both a print copy and a PDF. Each copy of Mirror Neuron costs about $8 to print and mail. If you can afford to, please consider paying that amount, or buy copies for strangers by paying more. Anyone who pays $8 or more will also receive a limited-edition print as a thank you from me.

I believe that everyone deserves access to art. If you agree, please consider chipping in to cover the costs for those who can't. We're all in this together, so let's be kind to each other. ♥

After 30 April, the cost of a copy will be $12 — assuming there are any left!

I hope you enjoy reading Mirror Neuron.
