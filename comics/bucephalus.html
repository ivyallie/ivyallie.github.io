--- title: Bucephalus supertitle: Comics layout: default permalink: /comics/bucephalus/ description: A gag comic about a spaceship that's actually just a boat. ---

These strips were originally published in the University of Connecticut's student paper, The Daily Campus, in fall of 2010, under the pseudonym K.X. Ellia. I was paid $5 per strip, which for many years afterward remained the most money I'd ever made by drawing comics. The art is a bit sloppy but I still think this came out pretty fun. I did one other comic strip for the Daily Campus, Past Tense, which you will not find online on account of its not being very good.
